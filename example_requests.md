# Example Public Records Requests

Here are several examples you can copy and paste into the **"Records Requested"** textbox within the Interactive Mode Dashboard to test the manual generation. The jurisdiction and correct legal citations will be automatically injected into your generated letter based on the agency you select!

## Federal Agencies (e.g., USDA, FDA)

### 1. USDA Meat Inspection Reports
> "Copies of all Noncompliance Records (NRs), Form 5400-4, and the associated Memorandum of Interview (MOI) reports for the federally inspected slaughterhouse establishment [Establishment Number] from January 1, 2023 to present."

### 2. FDA Import Refusals
> "All inspection reports (EIRs), warning letters, and refusal-of-admission records concerning pesticide residues on imported fresh produce from [Insert Importer/Country] issued during fiscal years 2022, 2023, and 2024. Please include laboratory analytical results associated with each refusal."

---

## State Agencies

### 1. California (e.g., CA Department of Food and Agriculture)
> "Copies of all dairy farm inspection reports, notices of violation, corrective action orders, and correspondence regarding untreated wastewater runoff for dairy operations located within Tulare County, California for the period January 1, 2023 through December 31, 2024."

### 2. Texas (e.g., TX Department of State Health Services)
> "All foodborne illness outbreak investigation reports, epidemiological summaries, laboratory confirmation records, and correspondence with county health departments related to outbreaks investigated by the DSHS Consumer Protection Division in Travis County from January 1, 2023 through December 31, 2024."

### 3. New York (e.g., NY Department of Agriculture and Markets)
> "Copies of all sanitary inspection reports, consumer complaint files, and notices of violation relating to [Insert Business Name], located at [Insert Address], for the past 5 years. I also request any internal correspondence discussing enforcement actions against this facility."

---

## India (Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare)

### 1. Pesticide Registrations
> "Under Section 6(1) of the RTI Act, 2005, I request the following:
> 1. Certified copies of the registration certificates for all pesticides approved by the CIB&RC under the Insecticides Act, 1968 between April 1, 2023 and March 31, 2024.
> 2. All technical evaluation reports and committee meeting minutes pertaining to the conditional approval of [Insert Pesticide Name]."

### 2. Agriculture Subsidies (Information on Beneficiaries)
> "Under Section 6(1) of the RTI Act, 2005, please provide:
> 1. The total amount of funds disbursed under the PM-KISAN scheme in the district of [District Name] during the financial year 2023-2024.
> 2. Guidelines and criteria used to identify ineligible beneficiaries and the mechanism employed to recover funds from them."
